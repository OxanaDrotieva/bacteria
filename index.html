Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Many bacterial species are not well-studied and many more remain unknown to science. Our role is to discover and document these bacteria. In particular, we are interested in bacterial species that have ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. We hypothesize these bacteria can be found on human body, to be more concise, In human belly button or at least, in someone’s belly button.
To test our hypothesis, we’ve sampled the navels of people across the country to identify bacterial species that colonize in our belly buttons. Each person’s identity is anonymous, they’ve instead been assigned an ID number. We’ve built the dashboard so both our research participants and fellow researchers can access. You can select any ID number and discover what bacterial species live in this participant’s navels.
